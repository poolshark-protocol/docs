A decentralized protocol for
directional liquidity

Poolshark is an open-source AMM protocol that makes it easy for users to catch directional moves from the comfort of a liquidity pool.

Get started

Get started with a single position

View your Uniswap v3 Position NFTs to quickly get started hedging potential impermanent loss, or average into positions when the price enters your range.

Poolshark provides Cover and Price pools for users to express their desire to enter or exit a directional position over some range. Range pools support bidirectional liquidity and offer default positions for truly passive LPing.

Trade your favorite tokens with price priority to catch sharp movements in price or set a long-term price target. All with the security of an immutable and permissionless on-chain exchange.
