    Your platform for mainstream
    transactions on Ethereum

    PoolSharks DCEX is an open source web3 protocol that makes it easy for users, protocols, and DAOs to reduce gas fees on Uniswap, Aave, and more.

    Get started

    Get started with a single transaction

    Run a single transcation to quickly get started pooling gas with other users on Ethereum using the latest non-custodial trustless infrastructure.

    Instead of storing data on-chain and forcing users to pay heavy gas costs, PoolSharks provides decentralized services to process user requests to spend token together from a single contract. The necessary pieces are already there to swap your favorite tokens and interact with your favorite DeFi protocols. Focus your time on coordinating with other users instead of worrying about gas costs.
